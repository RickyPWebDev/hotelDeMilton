Facillities Page

Spa Nava

Our Spa offers massages, facials, steam room, and swimming pool. A full 15 piece gym. Please come down and visit us on arrival for more information.

Cambell park and Cricket ground

Cambell park is full of lovely walks, we have a beautiful picturesque lake. The park has tennis courts, a children's playground and picnic benches. We are next door to the cricket ground make sure to book your matchday tickets to not miss out on matchdays. Families will want to take advantage of our Gullivers Land theme park, we offer a shuttle bus service that runs hourly 7am - 7pm Mon-Sun.

Parking

Free onsite parking for all guests. If your room includes premium parking a member of staff will greet you on arrival and direct you to your suite.

Room service

24 hour room service is available with food avaible from our sports bar. Please see the menu in your rooms for furthure details.
